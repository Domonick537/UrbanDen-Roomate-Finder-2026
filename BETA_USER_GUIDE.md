# UrbanDen Beta User Guide

Welcome to the UrbanDen beta! Thank you for helping us test and improve our roommate-finding platform. This guide will help you get started and make the most of your experience.

## What is UrbanDen?

UrbanDen is a modern roommate-finding platform designed to help you discover compatible roommates through an intuitive swipe-based interface. Our goal is to make finding the perfect roommate as easy and safe as possible.

## Getting Started

### 1. Creating Your Account

- Sign up with your email address
- Create a strong password
- Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

### 2. Setting Up Your Profile

A complete profile increases your chances of finding great matches:

- **Profile Photo**: Upload a clear, recent photo of yourself
- **Personal Information**: Fill in your name, age, and occupation
- **About Me**: Write a brief description about yourself and what you're looking for
- **Lifestyle Preferences**: Share your habits, schedule, and living preferences
- **Budget**: Set your monthly rent budget
- **Move-in Date**: Specify when you're looking to move

### 3. Setting Your Preferences

Configure who you'd like to see:

- Age range
- Gender preference
- Budget range
- Location preferences
- Lifestyle compatibility factors

## Finding Roommates

### Swiping

- **Swipe Right**: Like someone you'd be interested in living with
- **Swipe Left**: Pass on profiles that don't match your needs
- **Tap to View Full Profile**: See detailed information before deciding

### Matching

- When someone you liked also likes you back, it's a match!
- You'll receive a notification and can start chatting immediately
- Both users must like each other to create a match

## Communication

### Chat Features

- Send text messages to your matches
- Share photos (coming soon)
- Set up in-person meetings through the app
- Enable/disable read receipts in settings

### Safety Tips for Messaging

- Keep initial conversations within the app
- Don't share sensitive personal information early
- Report suspicious behavior immediately
- Trust your instincts

## Safety Features

### Identity Verification

Get verified to build trust with potential roommates:

1. Go to Settings > Identity Verification
2. Upload a photo ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID)
3. Take a verification selfie
4. Wait for admin approval (usually within 24-48 hours)

### Reporting and Blocking

If you encounter inappropriate behavior:

1. Tap the menu icon on any profile or chat
2. Select "Report User"
3. Choose a reason and provide details
4. Our team will review within 24 hours

To block a user:
1. Open their profile or chat
2. Tap the menu icon
3. Select "Block User"

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Can't Upload Photos
- Check that the app has permission to access your photos
- Ensure your photos are under 5MB
- Try a different image format (JPEG or PNG)

#### Not Seeing Any Matches
- Complete your profile fully
- Adjust your preferences to be more inclusive
- Check that your location services are enabled

#### Messages Not Sending
- Check your internet connection
- Try closing and reopening the app
- Contact support if the issue persists

## Privacy and Data

### What We Collect
- Profile information you provide
- Photos you upload
- Messages within the app
- Usage data to improve the platform

### What We Don't Share
- Your email address (without permission)
- Your exact location
- Your messages with other users
- Your verification documents (stored securely)

### Data Control
- Export your data: Settings > Data & Privacy > Export Data
- Delete your account: Settings > Account Actions > Delete Account

## Best Practices

### Creating a Great Profile

1. Use a clear, friendly photo
2. Write an honest and detailed bio
3. Be specific about your needs and preferences
4. Highlight what makes you a good roommate
5. Complete all sections of your profile

### Finding Compatible Roommates

1. Be honest about your lifestyle and habits
2. Ask important questions early
3. Discuss finances and responsibilities
4. Meet in public places first
5. Trust your instincts

### Meeting Potential Roommates

1. Always meet in public places first
2. Tell a friend or family member about your plans
3. Keep your phone charged and accessible
4. Don't share your home address until you're comfortable
5. Consider bringing a friend to initial meetings
